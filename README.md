**Business Impact & Key Insights**

1. Discounted sales contribute ~56% of total profit, indicating effective promotional strategies

2. London and New York are the strongest markets by sales and profit

3. Products like Laptops, Backpacks, and Shoes are consistent profit drivers

4. Q4 and Autumn show peak performance, highlighting seasonality

5. Clear identification of top-performing sales personnel for rewards and training
